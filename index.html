Waste Management in Tamil Nadu

Tamil Nadu generates thousands of tons of waste every day—from household garbage to industrial and biomedical materials. With rapid urban growth and rising environmental concerns, the need for smarter, unified waste solutions is urgent.

Introducing TWMF

The Tamil Nadu Waste Management Forum (TWMF) is a collaborative platform that brings together industries, urban local bodies, experts, and communities to transform how waste is managed across the state.

Solutions can help prevent hazardous waste leaks and improper disposal.
Solutions can help mitigate pollution and reduce the impact of climate change in Tamil Nadu.
Solutions can help create jobs and promote sustainable livelihoods in Tamil Nadu.

What We Focus On

Energy & Infrastructure

Developing smarter waste systems, sustainable energy solutions, and robust infrastructure for efficient waste management.

Community Awareness

Promoting public education, awareness campaigns, and engagement programs to encourage responsible waste disposal practices.

Policy & Innovation

Supporting policy frameworks, encouraging sustainable innovations, and fostering commercial solutions for waste management.

Circular Economy

Promoting residential participation in recycling, resource recovery, and circular economy practices to reduce environmental impact.
